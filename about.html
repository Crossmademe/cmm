About Cross Made Me

Who We Are

The Cross Didn't Just Save Me. It Made Me.

Cross Made Me Ministries started with a simple conviction: salvation isn't the finish line, it's the starting point. Too many people hear the Gospel, believe it, and then get left to figure out the rest on their own.

We're not a church, and we don't have a building. We're an outreach and discipleship ministry—we go to where people are: on the street through cross walks, at community events, and directly alongside people facing hard seasons, whether that's a need for food, prayer, or help with bills.

We exist to close the gap between believing in Christ and being built by Him. That means real discipleship, real community, and real accountability—not a program you check into once a week, but a walk we take with you.

Every cross walk, every outreach, and every conversation is aimed at the same goal: helping people encounter Christ and let the cross do what it actually does—transform, not just rescue.
