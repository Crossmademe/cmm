Who We Are

The Cross Didn't Just Save Me. It Made Me.

Cross Made Me Ministries started with a simple conviction: salvation isn't the finish line, it's the starting point. Too many people hear the Gospel, believe it, and then get left to figure out the rest on their own.

We exist to close that gap—walking alongside men and women as they move from believing in Christ to being built by Him. That means real discipleship, real community, and real accountability, not just a Sunday service.

Every event we host, every resource we share, and every conversation we have is aimed at the same goal: helping people encounter Christ and let the cross do what it actually does—transform, not just rescue.
