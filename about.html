Who We Are

The Cross Didn't Just Save Me. It Made Me.

Every ministry has an origin story. Ours starts with a simple truth—the cross didn't just save us. It made us.

Cross Made Me Ministries was founded in 2018 with one purpose: to spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ and create a community where people from all walks of life could find peace, compassion, and spiritual direction.

We're not a church, and we don't have a building. We're an outreach and discipleship ministry—we go to where people are: on the street through cross walks, at community events, and directly alongside people facing hard seasons, whether that's a need for food, prayer, or help with bills.

We believe transformation is real. We've lived it. And we exist to help others experience it too.

Saved by Grace. Made with Purpose. Sent to Impact.

This is just the beginning of our story. Stay connected as we share more about the journey, the mission, and the people who make C3M what it is.
