What We Do

Our Programs

Community Outreach & Ministry Events

The Community Outreach & Ministry Events Program focuses on building connections and providing hope through faith-based engagement. We host regular public events and initiatives designed to offer encouragement, tangible resources, and spiritual support to individuals and families.

Our outreach efforts include:

  • Community Gatherings & Events: Focused outreach initiatives that bring people together, foster fellowship, and strengthen local networks.
  • Cross Walking Ministries: Active, faith-first community engagement and street-level ministry.
  • Resource Distribution: Organizing the logistics and distribution of donated goods, supplies, and essential materials directly to those who need them most.

Community Support & Assistance Program

Cross Made Me Ministries provides direct assistance to individuals and families experiencing significant financial hardship. Our mission is to meet immediate, essential needs while actively working to improve the overall quality of life within our local community.

Through this program, we offer critical resources and relief services, including:

  • Essential Provisions: Food, clothing, and household necessities.
  • Financial Aid & Emergency Relief: Direct assistance for utility bills, transportation costs, and urgent financial crises.
  • Community Support Systems: Dedicated, immediate aid designed to help families stabilize and thrive during difficult times.

Discipleship, Prayer & Hope

At Cross Made Me Ministries, we believe that lasting transformation comes through genuine relationships, consistent encouragement, and the hope found in Jesus Christ. Our mission extends beyond meeting immediate physical needs—we strive to walk alongside individuals and families as they navigate life's challenges and grow in their faith.

Our Discipleship, Prayer & Hope initiative is built on creating meaningful connections that encourage people long after an outreach event has ended.

Through this ministry, we offer:

  • Personal Discipleship: Building authentic relationships through Bible-centered conversations, mentorship, accountability, and encouragement for those seeking to grow in their walk with Christ.
  • Prayer & Encouragement: Standing with individuals and families through prayer during seasons of hardship, uncertainty, illness, financial struggles, or personal challenges. We believe no one should face life's battles alone.
  • Hope Through Christ: Sharing the message of Jesus through everyday conversations, community outreach, and personal relationships. Our desire is to remind every person that hope is available, no matter where they are in life.
  • Connection & Community: Helping individuals build lasting relationships through Cross Walks, outreach events, volunteer opportunities, and ongoing fellowship that strengthens both faith and community.

At Cross Made Me Ministries, our goal is simple: serve people, love people, and point people to Jesus. Whether we're providing practical assistance, walking beside someone in prayer, or serving our community through outreach, every opportunity is a chance to demonstrate the love of Christ in action.
