Our Mission

Saved By Grace. Made With Purpose. Sent To Impact.

C3M — Cross Made Me Ministries exists to serve people from all walks of life by creating an environment filled with peace, compassion, and spiritual direction.

We believe in cherishing and strengthening the institution of the family—a vital foundation for building a culture that flourishes.

Our purpose is to share the truths of the Gospel with all who visit the Cross, and to preserve traditional values in today's society. We affirm the dignity of all people to live as Jesus taught—people who will in turn impact their families, their community, and our world.
