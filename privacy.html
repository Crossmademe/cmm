Legal

Privacy Policy

Cross Made Me Ministries respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including your name, phone number, email address, and message when you submit a form, request prayer, or contact us through our website.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to your message or prayer request
  • Share event and ministry updates
  • Follow up on inquiries about getting connected
  • Provide support and answer questions
  • Improve your experience with our website

SMS Communication

By submitting your information through our website forms, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Cross Made Me Ministries regarding events, prayer requests, and ministry updates.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance. Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of participation. We do not share your phone number or SMS opt-in data with third parties or affiliates.

Information Sharing

Cross Made Me Ministries does not sell, rent, or share your personal information, including mobile phone information, with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. We do not use tracking technologies to collect personal information for SMS marketing purposes.
